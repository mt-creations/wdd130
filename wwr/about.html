Helping you discover nature in a whole new way! We know these waters backwards and forwards and we love sharing our love of this great river with all of you!

History

We started as a family of rafting enthusiasts. For years we would take friends and family along on our adventures. Before long, we realized that we wanted EVERYONE to experience the thrill and joy of connecting to nature through white water rafting. Since opening our business in December of 1984 we have lead over 5000 people on amazing adventures.
